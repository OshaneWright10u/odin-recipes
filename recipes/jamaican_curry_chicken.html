jamaican curry chicken

Description

Jamaican Curry Chicken is a delightful and aromatic dish that brings the flavors of the Caribbean to your table. The combination of traditional Jamaican curry spices, tender chicken, and creamy coconut milk creates a rich and savory experience. This recipe balances the heat of curry with the sweetness of coconut milk, resulting in a flavorful and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs (1.13 kg) chicken, cut into pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp Jamaican curry powder
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp Jamaican curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

    1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and Jamaican curry powder.
    2. Sear chicken in a hot pan until browned. Set aside.
    3. Saute onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
    4. Stir in Jamaican curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
    5. Return seared chicken to the pan and coat with the curry mixture.
    6. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
    7. Add diced potatoes and carrots. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
    8. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
    9. Garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with rice or roti.
