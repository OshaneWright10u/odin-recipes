Jamaican Curry Chicken

Description

Jamaican Curry Chicken is a staple dish in Caribbean cuisine, known for its robust flavors and aromatic spices. This dish combines tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, spicy curry sauce infused with thyme, allspice, and Scotch bonnet pepper. The result is a deliciously warm and comforting meal that is perfect for any occasion. It’s traditionally served with white rice or rice and peas, making it a hearty and satisfying dish​
